Game records

\n \n

For other sources of game records, see the \nlist in the links section.\n

\n\n

On this page, you can download game records of top amateur games played on\nthe K Go Server (KGS, formerly known as the\nKiseido Go Server). I am grateful to Bill Shubert, who created KGS, for the\npermission to use these files, and for making them available to me in an easy\nway.

\n\n

The games in the archives below are those where either one of the players\n(or both) is 7d or stronger, or both are 6d. All comments are stripped from\nthese games, and all games with variations are omitted. They are suitable for\nuse with Kombilo.\n

\n\n\n

Need still more games? Have a look at the KGS games played by 4d+ players.

\n\n

There are several versions of each archive, compressed in different ways:\na .tar.gz, .tar.bz2, and .zip version; please choose the one which is most\nsuitable for you. The content of the uncompressed archives is completely\nidentical.
